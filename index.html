Discorver Teddy Riner

Judo 🥋

Member of the Paris Saint Germain Judo club Teddy started judo at five, he is now a sixth-dan black belt. He is the judoka with the most world championship titles! He was undefeated during 154 fights! A reference in judo.

His social commitment 🤝

Teddy Riner is the godfather of the Imagine Institute of Genetic Diseases, he accompanies sick children, doctors and researchers in their fight for life. He is also Ambassador of UNICEF France.

The Teddy Riner Academy 🏫

Like everything, Teddy has his training method and an infallible rigor. Now, it will be possible to learn part of the Teddy Riner method thanks to his Academy, it is here that he coaches future champions !
